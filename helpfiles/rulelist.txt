Displays the list of implemented condition handlers.
Usage: <cmd> [condition handlers]
Valid condition handlers: COMMON, MP3, 0DAY, MVID, NFO, TV, IMDB
Examples:
<cmd> <-- lists all rules
<cmd> COMMON <-- lists the default rules which can be used for every condition handler
<cmd> MP3 <-- lists MP3 specific rules
<cmd> MP3 <-- lists TV specific rules
