Usage: <cmd> sourcesite destsite sourcesection [destsection]
Copys the rules in Sourcesection from Sourcesite to Destsite.
If Destsection is defined the rules will be copied into a
section by that name. If it is not defined Destsection will
be set to Sourcesection.
Example: <cmd> HQ1 RACE1 MP3
         <cmd> HQ1 HQ1 MP3 FLAC
         <cmd> HQ1 HQ1 TV720 TV720WEB
