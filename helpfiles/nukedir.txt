Usage: <cmd> sitename section dir
Description: sets dir of section on site for nuking.
To remove a nuke section, omit dir parameter.
You can use the following macros in dir name:
<yyyy>: year, four digits
<yy>: last two digits of year
<mm>: month, two digits
<dd>: day of month, two digits
Example: <cmd> MyHQ MP3 /MP3/<yyyy>-<mm><dd>
See also: <cmdprefix>nuke
