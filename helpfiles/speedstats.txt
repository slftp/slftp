Usage: <cmd> sitename [SECTION] [RELEASENAME]
Displays the speed stats of your sites, sorted from lower to higher speed.
If you use with releasename, you need to specify the section!

Example: <cmd> HQ1
Example: <cmd> HQ1 GAMES
Example: <cmd> HQ1 BLURAY The.Ottoman.Lieutenant.2017.COMPLETE.BLURAY-LAZERS
