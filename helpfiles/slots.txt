Usage: <cmd> sitename1,sitename2,sitename3 [newslots]
Change number of slots of a site.
Examples:
<cmd> * <- shows all slots for each site
<cmd> HQ1 6 <- sets slots count to 6 for HQ1
<cmd> HQ1,HQ2,HQ3 3 <- sets slots count to 3 for HQ1. HQ2 and HQ3
<cmd> * 2 <- sets slots count to 2 for all sites

You can use the special sitename * as a wildcard for all sites.
